How to Build Your First AI Automation — No Coding Required

This guide is for AI beginners who want to get useful stuff done — without writing a single line of code.

🔍 What is an AI Automation?

Think of AI automation as a tiny assistant that runs on a schedule or trigger. It uses ChatGPT (or other models) to do boring tasks for you — like summarizing emails, rewriting text, or responding to feedback. And yes, you can build it with zero technical background.

📌 Example Use Case: Summarize Customer Reviews

Imagine you receive dozens of product reviews per day. With a short script, you can auto-summarize these into key takeaways:

"Summarize this product review with pros, cons, and suggestions: {{review_text}}"

We'll now build an automation that does this using Google Sheets + OpenAI + free automation tools.
